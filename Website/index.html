funagrams

classic tile game

Pick the right alphabets to solve the anagram. Tickles your grey matter. This game helps improve your vocabulary and might make you a spell bee. Have FUN!!!

Scramble

is the word tough to crack? don't worry we have got you covered without losing a point. use scramble to give you flash of the answer by scrambling the remaining alphabets.

Hint

scramble didn't help so much? you can trade-off your points with an insider. yes, hint can tell you the right position for the alphabet. you can use limited times based on mode.

Mode

gradually sharpen your skill. if you think Beginner is a piece of cake you should try Intermediate, like wise to Expert next. each level is not the same every time you play.
